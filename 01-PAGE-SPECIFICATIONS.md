## BLOG

### Main Blog
- **Source:** `Blog Writers Introductory_packet.docx`, `Blog Post Templates.docx`
- Grid layout with featured post
- Category filters

### Content Types (from Writer Packet)
1. **STEM Explainers** -- break down concepts (circuits, ML, biomedical devices)
2. **How-To & Skill Guides** -- start a STEM project, prepare for science fair
3. **Opportunity Spotlights** -- free programs, scholarships, competitions
4. **Personal Pathways & Stories** -- journey into STEM, lessons from failure
5. **Current STEM Developments** -- translate news for high schoolers

### Blog Post Templates (from Blog Post Templates.docx)
- **Template 1: Current Developments** -- Overview, What's Happening, Why This Matters, STEM Concepts, Future Implications, How Students Can Get Involved
- **Template 2: Technical How-To** -- Objective, Background Concepts, Step-by-Step, How It Works, Common Issues, Extensions, Real-World Applications

### Writing Guidelines (from Writer Packet)
- Tone: clear, encouraging, human
- Reading level: Grades 8-10
- Length: 600-1,200 words
- Short paragraphs, clear headers, bullet points
- Must be original, factually accurate, cite sources

### Writer Outreach
- **Source:** `Advertising Blog Posts Template.docx`
- LinkedIn outreach template for recruiting writers
- Email template for recruiting writers
- Target: high school students interested in engineering/robotics/STEM
- Offer: publication credit, service experience, community impact
